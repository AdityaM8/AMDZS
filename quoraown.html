How fast is Goku?
Chris Bennett, works at Unemployment
Updated Jul 24

Snake Way. 625,000 miles long and it takes Goku 177 days to reach the end. On his way back it takes him roughly 28 hours (24 hours after being revived, 1 hour since the battle started, and the 3 hour intermission), he's wished back to life on November 2nd around Midday based on the sunlight in the background. Nappa and Vegeta arrive on Earth the following day at 11:43, the battle starts at 12:20, the battle lasts for at least an hour before Vegeta calls the 3 hour intermission, so based on the sunset in the background Goku must arrive at the battlefield between 16:20 and 16:30 because that's when the sun sets in November time. So it took him roughly 28 hours. On his way back he flies over Snake Way instead of running along it. Since he flies over it I cut the distance in half.

312,500/28 = 11,160.714285714

11,160.714285714 mph is roughly Mach 14.6619998499 and this is only at a power level of 5,000. He doesn't reveal his true power until he battles Nappa.

11,160.714285714/5 = 2,232.1428571428

2,232.1428571428 mph is roughly Mach 2.93239997 which is only at a power level of 1,000. So for every 1,000 in his power level his speed increases by 2,232.1428571428 mph. This also goes for every other Z-Fighter/Z-Villain.

416 = 928.5714285714 mph
5,000 = 11,160.714285714 mph
8,000 = 17,857.142857142 mph
12,000 = 26,785.714285713 mph
16,000 = 35,714.285714284 mph
24,000 = 53,571.428571427 mph
32,000 = 71,428.571428569 mph
90,000 = 200,892.85714285 mph
180,000 = 401,785.7142857 mph
3 Million = 6,696,428.5714284 mph
30 Million = 66,964,285.714284 mph
60 Million = 133,928,571.42856 mph
150 Million = 334,821,428.57142 mph

Conclusion:

So at 150 Million when Goku first transforms into a Super Saiyan he's capable of moving at 0.4989887162 times the speed of light. Once he reaches 300.608 Million he'd be capable of moving at Lightspeed. However this kind of level of power wouldn't start surfacing until Androids #17 & #18 come onto the scene.

I know we're told when Goku returns to Earth he learnt the Instant Translocation technique on Planet Yardrat and he claims it allows him to dematerialise and travel as a mass of light at 186,000 miles per second, but this has proven to be incorrect. Firstly I like to use the Ocean Dub name Instant Translocation because transmission means send to another place, and translocation means move to another place which makes a lot more sense since it allows him to move anywhere throughout the Universe, and it allows him to move between dimensions as long as he has an Energy signal to lock onto. Secondly if it only allowed Goku to move at lightspeed it would take him 8 minutes to reach the Sun, and over 15 years to reach the edge of the Solar System. Thirdly Beerus' Planet is light years away and when Frieza invaded Earth, Goku was able to return to Earth in a matter of seconds after Gohan gave him an Energy signal to lock onto, so what we're looking at here isn't lightspeed movement, it's Teleportation.

How does Saiyan Strength work in Dragon Ball? How primal is it? Why?
Poland Diesta, I.T Business System Analyst at IBM Philippines (2013-present)
Updated Tue

The planet of the saiyans have 10 times more gravity than Earth. Therefore, their strength, durability, speed etc are all 10 times greater than of an average human. Thus, you can equate that one saiyan = 10 persons combined. Thats how strong an average saiyan is.

Of course, not all saiyans have the same level of strengths. Just like humans, we all dont share the same level of endurance, strength, speed etc. However, general rule of thumb is that, one average saiyan is equivalent of 10 average persons combined. This is because their environment is 10 times superior than our environment. The logic is that, because 1 saiyan = 10 persons, you will probably need 10 persons just to restrained 1 saiyan or match him in a fight.

On top of becoming 10 times stronger than a human, saiyans are also superior in biology and in nature. Humans, unlike saiyans are not warrior types. Sure, there are some humans that can fight like a warrior like those martial artists (Tien, Yamcha, Kuririn etc), but not majority of us are warrior by nature. However, to saiyans, there is a warrior spirit on each of them regardless of how strong or weak a saiyan is. Not to mention that being a warrior is the epitome of career for them. Thats why discrimination in their planet is very, very big. If youre born weak, chances is that you will never become a warrior you want to be because you will be discriminated because of your power level. You will end up like an engineer working to saiyan technologies or will be sent to other planet if youre weak or your power level is low. But if you were born with a high-power level, then you will easily be trained to become a warrior for your planet. I short, if you are a saiyan, your fate is being judged the moment you were born.

Mostly, strong warrior saiyans can be considered bully and rotten. Those who are in power are naturally treated well in their planet and they feel entitled because they are strong. Think of it like here in real life, if youre born as a slave, then you will be treated as a slave because you were born poor. In Gokus case, he was born with low power level (a poor person here in our planet). That is why Goku was sent to another planet to conquer it. Unfortunately, Goku forgot this mission when he smashed his head in the mountains, forgetting the mission and only his warrior instinct as a saiyan was left into his brain.

A low power level saiyan is still considered powerful when it is sent to other planet like Earth. Therefore Kid Goku is naturally more powerful than an average human even as a kid because of his saiyan biology making him stronger than average human by default, but not that much stronger. In the past, a kid Goku was considered twice as strong as an ordinary human only when he was still a kid. How do I know that Goku is only twice as strong as a normal human when he was a kid? Because Gokus power level was only 10 at the beginning of Dragon Ball.

And we learned back in saiyan saga through Raditz that a human has an average power level of 5. Gokus power level as a kid was only 10. Making him only twice as strong of an average human. While Goku is considered stronger than an average human, this kind of power level is a trash and nothing to saiyan standards.

Goku became strong only through hard work. Through Ki, he was able to increase his power level slowly. From tens, to hundreds, to thousands, to hundred thousand and so on. To be honest, Goku being sent to Earth is Gokus greatest blessing. He might be treated as trash in his own planet, but because he was tutored by Earths greatest masters, he was able to polish his fighting abilities and developed his fighting skills.

Mastering Ki allowed Goku to surpass his bodys limits by using Ki to amplify his body. Combine that to his saiyan biology to get stronger and stronger as time passes, this benefited Goku to reach new heights.

Normally, a saiyan doesnt aim to become the strongest. Theyre a bunch of racist that if ever theyre already on top and superior to other race, they no longer train anymore and enjoy the life of being treated highly because theyre strong. For them becoming strong is equivalent of being rich here on Earth.

For Goku, this wasnt the case. Goku was heavily influenced by his masters to always aim for the top. Thing that Goku took by heart and still doing up until now. Aiming to become the best warrior of all. Challenging anyone he thinks worthy to measure his strength and surpass even his own self every time. Gokus greatest enemy can be considered himself.

When Goku trained in King Kais planet with the same gravity planet as planet Vegeta, Goku surpassed even the likes of Nappa who was already considered a first class saiyan warrior. A thing that will never happen if Goku grew up in planet Vegeta. Therefore Vegetas pride was hurt so much when Goku, a low breed saiyan class warrior, was able to beat him in their fight, teaching him that even a weak warrior can grow stronger and stronger if one works hard on it.

Eventually, Gokus passion lead him to challenge even those who are considered dreadful in their universe like the likes of Frieza and some of his powerful minions. This Gokus passion eventually affected Vegeta as well, by considering Goku his rival by proving himself that he is the best saiyan and not Goku. As time goes by, this passion drive both to become the strongest in their universe and became an asset for any cosmic threats.

So How does Saiyan Strength work in Dragon Ball? How primal is it? The answer lies in their genetics and biology combined that with their environment. Saiyan strength worked because theyre naturally stronger because of their bodies having the capability to survive naturally in a 10 times gravity environment, affecting their bodies and making their bodies physically superior than other race by default, and their natural-warrior biology to become more powerful (Zenkai) as they continue to hone their bodies. Combine that feature to their bloodline technique of to transform (i.e Oozaru form in full moon or Super Saiyan transformation that is a set of multipliers of their base forms), this allowed saiyans to become one of the most powerful race in the whole universe, if not in the multiverse. Frieza was right to get afraid on them.

However, dont use Goku or Vegeta as the basis of saiyan standards. Theyre quite special. Their dedication and passion to get strong are not that of an average saiyan. Theoretically, all saiyans can become like them, but it needs intense passion and dedication to train and reached what theyve accomplished. The idea is similar to us humans. Bill Gates is the richest man in the whole world, and theoretically, we can all become as rich as Bill Gates if we work hard too because like us, Bill Gates is just only a human. But unfortunately, that is easier said than done. Becoming as rich as Bill Gates is something not all humans can pull through easily despite of being in the same breed of species. However, it can be done, the potential is there, its not just as easy as it is to become one. Similarly, in the case of Goku and Vegeta, applying that logic to the saiyan race, if power level is considered wealth, you can consider Goku as the Bill Gates of all the saiyans, while Vegeta is their Steve Jobs.
